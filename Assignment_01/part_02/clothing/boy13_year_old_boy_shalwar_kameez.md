# Shalwar Kameez for 13-Year-Old Boy

## Design Overview
A contemporary interpretation of the traditional shalwar kameez designed specifically for a 13-year-old boy, blending cultural heritage with modern sensibilities suitable for his age group.

## Kameez Description
- **Length**: Mid-thigh length (approximately 28 inches) to accommodate active movement while maintaining modesty
- **Fit**: Regular fit with slightly tailored waist to flatter the young physique without being too loose
- **Sleeves**: Full-length sleeves with subtle tapering, featuring adjustable buttoned cuffs
- **Neckline**: Traditional straight collar with a subtle embroidered border in contrasting thread
- **Front Panel**: Vertical panel with a contemporary geometric embroidery pattern running from shoulder to hem
- **Fabric**: Lightweight cotton blend with 2% elastane for comfort and slight stretch

## Shalwar Description
- **Fit**: Regular fit with comfortable ease for movement and growth allowance
- **Waistband**: Elasticated waistband with drawstring for adjustable fit as the boy grows
- **Leg Opening**: Tapered leg with ribbed ankle cuffs for a modern, sporty look
- **Pockets**: Two side seam pockets and one secure zippered pocket on the right side
- **Fabric**: Matching cotton blend with reinforced stress points for durability

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep navy blue with silver-gray accent embroidery
- **Secondary**: Forest green with golden yellow embroidery detail
- **Seasonal Variant**: Light beige with deep brown embroidery for warmer months

## Embellishments
- Minimal embroidery on chest and sleeve cuffs using metallic thread
- Small decorative buttons at the placket in coordinating colors
- Subtle woven texture in the fabric for visual interest without excess ornamentation

## Styling Notes
Perfect for family gatherings, religious occasions, and cultural celebrations while remaining comfortable for the active lifestyle of a teenage boy. The design respects traditional aesthetics while acknowledging the preferences and mobility needs of a 13-year-old wearer.
